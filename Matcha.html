About This Tea:

Origin: Japan – known for its ancient tea ceremonies and meditative calm

Ingredients:

  • Matcha powder (culinary or ceremonial grade)
  • Hot water (not boiling)
  • Optional: milk (for lattes)
  • Optional: honey or sweetener
  • Optional: bamboo whisk (chasen)

Flavor Profile:

  • Grassy
  • Earthy
  • Umami

Why You'll Love It 💚

  • Super antioxidant-rich
  • Energy boost without the crash
  • Perfect for mindfulness and focus

🍵 Make It at Home

Preparing matcha is a peaceful ritual that creates a smooth and vibrant tea experience.

  • 🌡️ Heat water to ~80°C (not boiling).
  • 🥄 Sift 1–2 tsp of matcha powder into a bowl to remove clumps.
  • 🧴 Add a small splash of hot water and whisk vigorously in a zig-zag motion.
  • 🍃 Add the rest of the water and continue whisking until frothy.
  • 🥛 Optional: Add frothed milk for a matcha latte.
  • ☁️ Serve and enjoy the moment.
  • Use a bamboo whisk (chasen) for the best froth!
  • For a sweeter version, mix with vanilla or honey.
  • Try it warm with a spoon of honey and oat milk on quiet evenings 🍯🥛
