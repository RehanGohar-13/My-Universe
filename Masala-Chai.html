About This Tea:

Origin: India

Ingridiants:

  • Black tea leaves (Assam or Darjeeling)
  • Milk (whole or dairy-free)
  • Water
  • Fresh ginger
  • Cardamom pods
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Cloves
  • Black peppercorns (optional)
  • Sugar or jaggery (for taste)

Flavor Profile:

  • Spicy
  • Creamy
  • Warming

Why You'll Love It ❤️

  • Traditional and comforting
  • Great for digestion
  • Customizable spice levels

☕ Make It at Home

You can also brew this comforting chai at home with just a few simple steps:

  • 🔥 Boil 1 cup of water with ½ cup of milk in a small saucepan.
  • 🍃 Toss in 1 tsp of black tea leaves (Assam works best).
  • 🌶️ Crush some cardamom, ginger, cloves, and cinnamon — add them in.
  • ⏳ Let everything simmer for 3–5 minutes until the aroma fills your kitchen.
  • 🍯 Add sugar or jaggery to taste. Stir gently.
  • ☕ Strain into your favorite cup, breathe in, and sip slowly.
  • For a spicier kick, add a tiny pinch of black pepper!
  • ✨ Rehan’s Note
    I like mine extra sweet and warm on rainy days — try adding an extra spoon of sugar!
