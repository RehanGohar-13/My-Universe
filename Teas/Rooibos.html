🌿 Make It at Home

Rooibos is easy to brew and gentle on the body — here’s how to enjoy it:

  • 💧 Bring 1 cup of water to a gentle boil.
  • 🍂 Add 1–2 tsp of loose Rooibos or a tea bag.
  • ⏳ Let it steep for 5–7 minutes — the longer, the richer the flavor.
  • 🍯 Add honey or lemon if you like. You can even enjoy it with milk.
  • ☕ Pour into a cozy mug and sip slowly.
  • Try it cold as iced tea with orange slices — refreshing in summer!
